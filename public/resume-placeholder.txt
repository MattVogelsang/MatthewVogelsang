INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADDING YOUR RESUME:

1. Create or export your resume as a PDF file
2. Name it "resume.pdf" 
3. Place it in this "public" folder (same location as this file)
4. The download button in the Resume section will automatically work

The resume.pdf file should be placed in the public folder so it can be accessed at the root URL of your website.

Your resume PDF will be downloadable when users click the "Download Resume" button on your portfolio website.
